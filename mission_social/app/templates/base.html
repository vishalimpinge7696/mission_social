FAQ

  • Do I need to attend every meetup?

    No, it's not a compulsion for anyone to attend every meetup/workshop/competition. The community won't restrict anyone to skip any of the event if the topic of that particular workshop/meetup doesn't fall under his/her domains of interest. But you need to keep in mind that more you'll attend, better you'll learn.

  • Who will be the mentors for community?

    Community leaders will regularly keep on inviting various industrial people for guest lectures and support. Also community mentors will include professors and students (pursuing internship or remote job) who have good amount of knowledge in these domains.

  • Is joining this community free?

    Joining ML-AI Hub is absolutely FREE. Attending lectures, talks and meetups is also FREE. Entry to some workshops or competitions may be chargeable but again, the charges will be returned as prize money.
